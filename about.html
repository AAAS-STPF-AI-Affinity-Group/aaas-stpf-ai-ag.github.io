About Our Group

Our Mission

The AAAS Science & Technology Policy Fellows (STPF) AI Affinity Group brings together fellows who are interested in artificial intelligence and its applications in federal service. Our mission is to foster collaboration, share knowledge, and advance the responsible development and implementation of AI across government agencies.

Our Vision

We envision a future where AI technologies are developed and deployed in ways that benefit society, uphold democratic values, and advance scientific progress. By connecting fellows across different federal agencies, we aim to create a community of practice that can support thoughtful AI policy development and implementation.

History of the Group

The AI Affinity Group was established in 2022 by a small cohort of STPF fellows who recognized the growing importance of AI in federal science and technology policy. What began as informal discussions evolved into a structured community as interest in AI grew across fellowship placements.

Since our formation, we have organized workshops, speaker series, agency tours, and collaborative projects focused on AI ethics, governance, technical standards, and applications across various sectors including healthcare, energy, environment, and national security.

Group Activities

  • Monthly "Salon" Meetings: Regular gatherings to discuss AI developments relevant to federal service
  • Workshops: Hands-on learning sessions focused on AI tools and methodologies
  • Speaker Series: Talks by AI researchers, policymakers, and industry leaders
  • Agency Tours: Visits to agencies implementing AI solutions
  • Resource Development: Creating policy briefs, tutorials, and reference materials

Our Core Values

Ethical AI

We promote the development and use of AI systems that are fair, transparent, accountable, and aligned with human values.

Inclusivity

We believe in diverse perspectives and ensuring AI benefits all segments of society, especially underrepresented groups.

Innovation

We encourage creative solutions and novel approaches to complex policy challenges in the AI space.

Scientific Integrity

We uphold rigorous standards of evidence and scientific methods in all AI-related activities.

Collaboration

We foster partnerships across agencies, disciplines, and sectors to advance AI for public good.

Knowledge Sharing

We promote the open exchange of ideas, resources, and best practices to accelerate learning.
